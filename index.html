About us

Introducing IntelliDose, the intelligent solution designed to revolutionize pill management for seniors, kids and others for whom this process is routine.

With automated reminders, personalized schedules, and easy-to-use features, IntelliDose ensures that every dose is taken on time, bringing peace of mind to both treatment receivers and their caregivers.

Embrace a new era of health management with IntelliDose, where technology meets care, making medication adherence seamless and worry-free.

Features

  • Storage & recording. A handy storage that keeps track of the med stockpile.

  • Personalized schedule. Create different schedules for multiple users.

  • Seamless authentication. RFID tags usage to simplify device usage.

  • Logging & notifications. Be aware of all manipulations with the device.

  • Monitoring & signaling. Receive visual and audio notifications when your stock is low
